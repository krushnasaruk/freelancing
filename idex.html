Professional Background

Dr. Manisha Choure is a Consultant in Obstetrics and Gynecology at Vishwaraj Hospital, Loni Kalbhor, Pune. She is known for her compassionate approach and commitment to providing the highest quality of care to women from adolescence through menopause.

Her expertise covers antenatal and postnatal care, high-risk pregnancy management, gynecological consultations, infertility evaluation, and minimally invasive surgery. She believes in blending modern medical science with personalized attention for every patient.
